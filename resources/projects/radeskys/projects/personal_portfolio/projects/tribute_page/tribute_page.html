Here's a timeline of Commander Snow's life:

  • Season 1 - Lived with the Starks on Winterfell.
  • Season 2 - Went on to Castle Black to be a Ranger.
  • Season 3 - Went North of the wall to get intel on Wildlings.
  • Season 4 - Defended Castle Black from Wildling attacks.
  • Season 5 - Died in the hands of Unhappy Brothers of the Night's Watch.
  • Season 6 - Led army to war against the Boltons at Winterfell.
